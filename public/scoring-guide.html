Reviewer Rules
RULE 01
Price shapes the bar
A cheap snack that's "good" hits harder than a pricey one. The matrix accounts for this — use it.
RULE 02
Be honest, not generous
A 3 should genuinely make you want to text someone. If you're unsure, it's probably a 2.
RULE 03
Context matters
Rate it for what it's supposed to be — a convenience store snack vs a deli counter have different benchmarks.
RULE 04
Not everything qualifies
Disappointing products don't get a score — they're not a Legit Buy at any price. Fine/decent at cheap or fair price also doesn't make the cut. Only submit what genuinely earns a score.
RULE 05
3* is rare
A pricey product scoring 3 has to be genuinely extraordinary — the kind of thing people will thank you for.
RULE 06
One experience, one score
Score it fresh, on the day. Don't revisit to inflate. If it gets better over time, submit a follow-up.
